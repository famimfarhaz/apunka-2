**About College:**
Khulna Polytechnic Institute is an institution under the Directorate of Technical Education, Technical and Madrasa Education Division, Ministry of Education, Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh. It was established in 1963 with the aim of creating skilled human resources. The institution has 156 permanent revenue-funded positions and 40 temporary revenue-funded positions. At present, the diploma-in-engineering curriculum in a total of 9 technologies is being conducted here. Situated on 32.25 acres of land, the institute is located 4.5 kilometers away from Khulna Railway Station and the divisional bus terminal. Currently, a total of 1,200 students are admitted in 9 technologies each year, and about 4,500 students study across 4 semesters. It is a Registered Training Organization (RTO) under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board.

Khulna Polytechnic Institute is making every effort to spread technical education throughout the country, especially in the southern region of Bangladesh. Since Khulna is an industrial area, our institute offers nine relevant technologies to meet the demands of industries: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Power, Electronics, Computer, Environmental, IPCT (Instrumentation & Process Control Technology), and RAC (Refrigeration & Air Conditioning). Thus, our students become skilled in these fields. We always maintain strong connections with relevant industries and provide them with a database of our students after the completion of their final semester. College Clubs at Khulna Polytechnic Institute
🔹 BNCC – Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (KPI Unit)

The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) is a national youth organization that trains students in discipline, leadership, and patriotism through military-style activities. The KPI Unit serves as a branch of this national force, allowing students of Khulna Polytechnic Institute to participate in drills, parades, leadership training, and community service.
📩 Contact: https://web.facebook.com/kpibncc

🔹 Rovers & Scouts – KPI Unit

The Scouts and Rovers Movement is a global youth program focused on skill development, social service, and character building. The Rovers & Scouts Unit at KPI is a local branch of this worldwide organization, engaging students in camping, community volunteering, disaster response, and moral development activities.

🔹 Red Crescent Society – KPI Unit

The Bangladesh Red Crescent Society is part of the global Red Cross & Red Crescent humanitarian network. The KPI Branch actively participates in blood donation campaigns, disaster relief operations, first aid training, and social welfare activities. It plays a vital role in serving both the campus and the wider community.

🔹 Rangers – KPI Unit

The Rangers are the advanced wing of the Girl Guides/Scouting movement, designed for young women to develop leadership, social responsibility, and life skills. The KPI Rangers Unit empowers female students through community service, cultural events, training programs, and leadership-building initiatives, connecting them with the national and international Ranger movement.

🔹 ICT Training Cell – KPI Unit

The ICT Training Cell at KPI is a specialized branch focused on building students’ digital and technological skills. As part of the broader national ICT education initiative, this unit trains students in computer literacy, software applications, and technical skills that prepare them for the modern job market and technological innovations.
